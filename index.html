Welcome to PokemonLetsGoData!

This is a data visualization of Generation 1 Pokemon created with the D3 library. Mouse over a Pokemon to get a closer look at its stats. The colored buttons on the left can filter the Pokemon based on their types. The gray buttons change the sizes of the Pokemon based on their corresponding stats. That's pretty much all there is, so please enjoy!
